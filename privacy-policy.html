Privacy Policy

Effective date: November 2, 2025

Dashie ("we", "our", "the App") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy describes how we collect, use, store, and protect information when you use our application.

Information We Collect

When you use Dashie, we access the following information from your Google account:

  • Profile Information (profile scope): Your full name, profile picture, and any other personal information you've made publicly available on your Google account. This is used for authentication and personalization within the dashboard.
  • Email Address (email scope): Your primary Google account email address for account identification and authentication.
  • Google Calendar Data (https://www.googleapis.com/auth/calendar.readonly scope - Read-Only): We access calendar events from any calendar you can access through your Google Calendar, including your personal calendar and shared calendars. This includes event details such as title, time, location, description, and attendees. We also can see the email addresses of contacts or groups you share calendars with (as part of event attendee information). This data is used solely to display your schedule on the family dashboard.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely for the following purposes:

  • Display your calendar events within the Dashie dashboard interface.
  • Authenticate your identity and maintain your user session.
  • Store your dashboard preferences and settings (theme, widget configurations, etc.).
  • We do NOT: Share, sell, or transfer your personal information or calendar data to any third parties.
  • We do NOT: Use your data for advertising, marketing, or any purpose other than providing dashboard functionality.

Data Storage and Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • Encryption in Transit: All data transmitted between your device, our servers, and Google APIs uses HTTPS/TLS encryption.
  • Encryption at Rest: User settings and preferences stored in our database (Supabase) are encrypted at rest using AES-256 encryption.
  • Access Tokens: Google OAuth access tokens are encrypted and stored securely. Tokens are never logged or exposed in application code.
  • No Credential Storage: We do not store your Google account password. Authentication is handled entirely through Google's secure OAuth 2.0 protocol.
  • Minimal Data Storage: Calendar events are accessed in real-time from Google APIs and are not permanently stored on our servers. Calendar data may be cached temporarily to improve performance.
  • Secure Infrastructure: Our backend services run on Supabase (PostgreSQL) and Vercel, both SOC 2 Type II certified platforms with enterprise-grade security.

Data Retention and Deletion

We retain your data only as long as necessary to provide our services:

  • Calendar Data: We do not permanently store your calendar events. Data is retrieved directly from Google Calendar API on-demand. Cached calendar data is automatically purged when no longer needed.
  • User Preferences: Dashboard settings (theme, widget configurations, family name, location) are stored indefinitely while your account is active.
  • Authentication Tokens: OAuth refresh tokens are stored securely and retained until you revoke access or delete your account.
  • Account Deletion: You may delete your account at any time through the Account settings in the app, or by emailing jwlerch@gmail.com. Upon deletion:
    • All user settings and preferences are permanently deleted from our database within 30 days.
    • All OAuth tokens are immediately revoked and deleted.
    • Any cached calendar data is immediately purged.
    • We retain no copy of your calendar events after account deletion.
  • Revoking Access: You can revoke Dashie's access to your Google Calendar at any time through your Google Account Permissions page. Revoking access will prevent Dashie from accessing your calendar, but will not delete your Dashie settings. To delete all data, request account deletion.

Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties, except:

  • Service Providers: We use trusted third-party infrastructure providers (Supabase for database, Vercel for hosting) to operate our service. These providers have access to encrypted data solely for infrastructure operations and are contractually obligated to protect your data.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or governmental authority.

Your Rights and Control

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: You can view and export your Dashie settings from the Settings menu within the app.
  • Modification: You can update your settings, family information, and preferences at any time through the app.
  • Revocation: You can revoke Dashie's access to your Google Calendar at any time through Google Account Permissions.
  • Deletion: You can delete your account through the Account settings in the app, or by emailing jwlerch@gmail.com.
  • Data Portability: You can request an export of your Dashie settings data by emailing jwlerch@gmail.com.

OAuth Scopes Requested

Dashie requests the following Google OAuth scopes. We only request access to data necessary for dashboard functionality:

  • profile - Basic profile information (name, profile picture)
  • email - Your email address for account identification
  • https://www.googleapis.com/auth/calendar.readonly - Read-only access to your Google Calendar events

All scopes are read-only. Dashie cannot modify, delete, or create calendar events.

Children's Privacy

Dashie is designed for families but is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately at jwlerch@gmail.com.

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Effective date" at the top of this page. Continued use of Dashie after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy. We will notify users of material changes via email or in-app notification.
